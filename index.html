About Us

SEACON Properties was founded by before said 3 partners with diverse yet complementary backgrounds. Our first partner brings over 17+ years of marketing excellence in India and abroad, the second partner has a civil engineering proven track record spanning about 19+ years in developing residential as well as commercial projects successfully, and the third partner as a civil engineer boasts 24+ years of expertise in building and land development with a completion track record one of finest projects in world till now .

Vision and Mission

Mission :Our mission is rooted in delivering projects that not only meet market expectations but exceed them. We consider it as affordable selling prices with more returns for the investors when compared to usual market practice.
